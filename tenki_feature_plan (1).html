Core (all plans)
Auto-location on first launch
Free
Detects the user's city via IP on first open. No permission prompt needed — fallback to GPS after onboarding.
GET /v1/ip-lookup?ip=auto GET /v1/weather?lat&lon
Solves: "I don't know my exact coordinates." First weather shown in under 2 seconds, zero friction.
Live weather + 7-day forecast
Free
Hero card with current temp, condition, feels-like. Hourly scroll + 7-day area chart below. Sunrise/sunset tiles. Chance-of-rain progress bars for evening hours.
GET /v1/weather?days=7&ai=false GET /v1/hourly GET /v1/daily
Solves: the core product promise — accurate, glanceable weather for everyday decisions (carry an umbrella? go to market today?).
Multi-city location cards
Free
Save up to 5 cities. Each card shows local time, temp, wind, AQI badge. Pull-to-refresh updates all in parallel via Promise.all.
GET /v1/weather (per city, parallel)
Solves: checking weather for family in other counties, planning inter-city travel (e.g. Nanyuki → Nairobi day trip).
Monthly weather calendar
Free
react-native-calendars grid showing a weather emoji per day based on dominant condition. Tap a date to see a mini daily summary. Useful for planning events weeks ahead.
GET /v1/daily?days=7 (Pro: /v1/forecast14)
Solves: "Is there a good window to plant next month?" — free users get 7 days in the calendar, Pro unlocks 14.
Offline mode + MMKV cache
Free
All weather responses cached with TTL (current: 5 min, daily: 1 hr). When offline, stale data is shown with a soft "Cached data" banner. Background refreshes skip AI summaries with ?ai=false to preserve quota.
ai=false on background refresh
Solves: patchy data connectivity in rural areas like Laikipia — the app stays useful even with no signal.
Pro features
AI weather insight card
Pro
Gemini-powered summary card on the home screen below the forecast chart. Shows agronomic context (good day to spray? harvesting risk?), risk flags, and actionable advice in plain language. Supports Swahili (lang=sw) for East African users.
GET /v1/insights (Pro+)
Solves: raw numbers don't tell farmers what to do — this card translates humidity/wind/UV into a daily action plan.
14-day extended forecast
Pro
Unlocks a "14 Days" tab on the forecast switcher. Collapsible day rows with condition, high/low, rain probability. Calendar grid expands to fill two weeks.
GET /v1/forecast14 (Pro+)
Solves: planning market days, livestock movement, or planting schedules that need a two-week horizon — the 7-day window isn't enough.
Weather webhooks (rain alerts)
Pro
UI to register up to 10 webhook endpoints or push-notification targets. WeatherAI POSTs when rain/frost/wind conditions are met at saved coordinates. Settings tab shows active webhooks with a toggle per trigger type.
POST /v1/webhooks GET /v1/webhooks DEL /v1/webhooks/:id
Solves: farmers can't watch the app all day — push a rain warning to their phone or SMS relay before it's too late to cover crops.
Scale features
Forestry & tree analysis (Farmer Mode)
Scale
FAB (leaf icon) on Weather tab opens the Forestry sheet. Upload a drone/satellite farm image. Returns annotated overlay, tree count, density/acre, canopy %, health breakdown (healthy / needs care / replacement), and Gemini agronomic recommendations. History of past analyses stored in Firestore.
POST /v1/trees/analyze (multipart) GET /v1/trees/history GET /v1/trees/quota
Solves: farmers have no affordable way to assess tree/canopy health across large plots. A single drone pass + Tenki gives a full agronomic health report in under a minute.
SMS weather alerts (Bomet programme)
Scale + approval
Register farmers into the Bomet Agricultural Alert System. Daily SMS with condition summary and actionable advice. Admins can send targeted alerts (drought, frost, extreme wind) via predefined templates. Opt-in required. SMS stats dashboard in the admin settings panel.
POST /v1/sms/bomet/register POST /v1/sms/alert GET /v1/sms/stats GET /v1/sms/health
Solves: smallholder farmers without smartphones still need weather alerts — SMS reaches feature phones with no data plan.
Maps tab with nearby weather pins
Free
Full-screen dark map (react-native-maps). Pins for nearby cities from /v1/weather-geo. Tapping a pin opens a bottom sheet with a quick 3-day forecast. Search bar overlaid at top.
GET /v1/weather-geo?ip=auto
Solves: spatial awareness — see at a glance whether the rain is coming from the west or if Nairobi is clear when Nanyuki is overcast.

Locked feature treatment
Free users see Pro/Scale features with a soft purple lock overlay and an "Upgrade" banner. Tapping calls getPaystackConfig() Firebase callable and opens an in-app Paystack payment sheet. No hard walls — locked features are visible but inactive, which creates pull without frustration.

AI quota guard
Background refreshes always append ?ai=false to preserve AI request quota. AI is only called on foreground open and explicit "refresh" taps. Free plan gets 200 AI calls/month — the guard keeps the insight card available all month without burning quota on silent refreshes.

Upgrade flow
Locked features show a purple "Upgrade" banner. Tapping calls the getPaystackConfig() Firebase callable, gets the public key + plan prices (KES 2,500 Pro / KES 9,500 Scale), then opens a Paystack payment sheet in-app. On success, the plan badge in Settings updates and locked features unlock immediately without an app restart.
